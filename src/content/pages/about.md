Life is an endless sequence of negatives and positives, always converging to zero.

I aim for HOP (Human Oriented Programming) and write code — well, let the AI write it — that solves human problems. I'm drawn to new things across every field, and I prefer being handed resources and a goal over a detailed plan.

I enjoy collecting books more than reading them, and I read by picking from the collection. So this blog mixes in the books I've read and passing thoughts about as often as code. My most-used phrase is "that can happen too."

This blog (Between Negative and Positive) handles only the minimum information needed for visit statistics and advertising. Here are the essentials.

## Information Collected

This blog has no sign-up or comment features, so it does not directly collect personal information such as your name or email. The third-party services below, however, automatically collect visit data.

## Third-Party Services

- **Google Analytics**: Collects anonymous statistics such as visitor counts, page views, and approximate location and device information. It is used to understand which posts are read.
- **Google AdSense**: When ads are served, Google and its advertising partners may use cookies to show interest-based ads based on your previous visits.

Both services use cookies. You can block cookies in your browser settings or turn off personalized ads at [Google Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads). You can review how Google handles data at the [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites) page.

## Your Rights and Contact

If you want to access, correct, or delete your data, or have any privacy-related questions, email intzzzero@gmail.com. This policy may be updated as needed, and any changes are reflected in the date above.
